Nisaan Skyline

When thinking of Nissan GTR, the current R35 comes to mind.
But what made this model so famous was the Nissan Skyline GTR (R32),
dubbed "Godzilla" by the Australian press due to its Japanese origins.
The Nissan Skyline is a cult favourite since it has appeared
in many video games and films. The R32, R33, and R34 are the rarest models
right now and can't be easily found on used-car websites.
